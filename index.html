City Hall

Historical Background

Philadelphia's City Hall is a prominent landmark that began construction in 1871, taking nearly 30 years to complete.

Interesting Facts

  • No other city has a larger municipal building
  • John McArthur Jr, a Scottish immigrant, designed the building
  • When it was completed in 1901, City Hall was the world's tallest habitable structure. It held this title until 1908.

Visiting Hours

The City Hall Visitor Center is open Monday through Friday from 10 AM to 4 PM.
